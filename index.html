My Morals

Honesty

I really love to surround myself around people who are honest and people who will tell me what is on their mind because I believe that honesty is the best policy.

Communication

I believe that people can not get along if they don't communicate well and that is why I love to have team cultures when working with people for the first time.

Grateful

After a lot of self reflection I have realized that I sometimes tend to take things in my life for granted and that is why I keep a jounal of all the things I am most grateful for.

Loyality

I pride myself on being loyal person and I like to surround myself around people who are loyal because I have dreams that I want to make realities and I don't have time for fake friends.

Boss Mentality

This mindset really helped me build my confidence. It also humbles you because although you know that you are a boss, you don't let your ego get out of control.

Failure

Learning to love failure is an important mindset because those who are not comfortable failing never grow. I believe it is ok to fail as long as you don't fail the same way twice.

Family

I come from a really huge family and so family time is really important to me. I love my family and I owe everything I have and am to them. Family is the one thing that I would do anything for.
